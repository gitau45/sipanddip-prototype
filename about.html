Our Story

A hidden gem in Thika, where great food meets unforgettable experiences

Welcome to Sip and Dip Bistro

Nestled along the vibrant Thika-Mangu Flyover Road, Sip and Dip Bistro began with a simple vision: to create a space where friends, families, and colleagues could escape the ordinary and experience something truly special.

What started as a dream to bring together authentic Kenyan cuisine with world-class hospitality has grown into Thika's most beloved destination. From our signature nyama choma zone to our serene poolside dining, every corner of Sip and Dip is designed with you in mind.

We believe that great food brings people together. That's why we source our ingredients locally, work with the freshest produce, and prepare every dish with passion and precision. Our grill masters have perfected the art of nyama choma, while our chefs create mouthwatering dishes that keep our guests coming back.

But Sip and Dip is more than just a restaurant. It's a place to unwind by the pool, dance the night away in our underground club, catch the big game on our massive screens, or simply enjoy quality time with loved ones. We've created a space where everyone feels welcome, and every visit feels like a celebration.
